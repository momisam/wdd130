Lagos | Nigeria

Lagos State, located in southwestern Nigeria, is the country’s smallest state by land area but the most populous and economically vibrant. It is often referred to as the commercial capital of Nigeria because it hosts the nation’s busiest seaport, a thriving business hub, and a wide range of industries. The state is famous for its cultural diversity, modern skyline, and bustling lifestyle, with Lagos city often described as “the city that never sleeps.” Despite challenges like traffic congestion and rapid urbanization, Lagos remains a center of opportunity, creativity, and growth, attracting people from across Nigeria and beyond.
